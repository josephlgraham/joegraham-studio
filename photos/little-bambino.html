Little Bambino
📍 Cardiff, Alabama  ·  📷 Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra  ·  August 21, 2024

Hidden within a tangled canopy of serrated leaves and sprawling vines, a 'Little Bambino' watermelon nears its peak ripeness. This icebox variety is bred for compact size that belies its intense sugar content, ripening in the humid microclimate of a Southern garden.
