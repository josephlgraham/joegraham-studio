About Me
Who Am I

I grew up exploring the woods of Alabama. I learned to shoot before I could drive a truck. My wanderlust started at a young age when I was introduced to the big world we live in through my grandfather's National Geographic magazines.

I have been blessed to have a career that gets me around to so many interesting places.

I'm a sucker for a national park.

Solar punk enthusiast. Occasional woodworker. Full-time noticer of things.

Who I'd Like to Work With

Brands with taste. Graffiti artists that want to document their work. Anyone building something real. If the project is authentic, chances are I'm interested. I'm available throughout central Alabama, but up for some travel if the opportunity presents itself. If you've got something worth shooting, let's talk.

Who I'd Like to Meet

People shooting film. People who are still building things with their hands. People who care more about their communities than their follower count. And especially people like me that are here to be bridgemakers into this brave new world.
